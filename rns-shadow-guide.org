* Intro
Rabbit & Steel Shadow Class guide written primarily for v2.0.2.7. 

At the time of starting this guide, the author (Quoise) has 11 Multiplayer (MP) Lunar wins and 20 Hard wins on Shadow.
Primarily, the recommendations here are targetted as a carry at 4P Lunar and high WR consistency.

This guide could not be made possible without the help of other players in mino_dev Discord.

** Sample Definitions
- {{{abls(N1)}}}, {{{gems(O1)}}}, {{{gems(S1)}}}, {{{gems(R1)}}}, {{{gems(E1)}}}, {{{gems(G1)}}} :: _Normal_, _Opal_, _Sapphire_, _Ruby_, _Emerald_ and _Garnet_ variants of Primary respectively.
- {{{gems(R1)}}}, {{{gems(R2)}}}, {{{gems(R3)}}}, {{{gems(R4)}}} :: _Ruby_ variants of each respectively: Primary, Secondary, Special and Defensive.
- BiS :: Best in Slot; universal best upgrade.
- AoE :: Area-of-Effect; typically used in context of hitting multiple enemies, e.g. fights in Churchouse or Sanctum.
- oGCD :: Shadow's S3, S4; these can be used without sacrificing GCD time spent on other abilities.
- iframe :: Invincibility

* Playstyle
** Base Kit
Maximum damage rotation would prioritize in order of availability.

\(N4 \to N3 \to N1 \to N1\)

More typically, for mobile targets, I save N3 for when I'm out of range or shackled.
N4 is good for burst, i.e. on 4P Lunar against the unshielded butterfly on (Geode) Plee or (Sanctum) Capricorn.

As a rule-of-thumb, N1/N4 and N2 have about the same hitbox horizontally.
[fn:N2:Diameter of N1 and N4 is 5% bigger than the length of N2.]

* Gems Tier List
Tier placing prioritizes "lines" and WR consistency. Shadow, by gem design, should buy a gem first gem first shop, most are damage positive.
