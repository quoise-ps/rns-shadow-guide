- NX = Normal (non-gem) variant of X ability

Tier placing prioritizes "lines" and WR consistency. Shadow, by gem design, should
always buy a gem first gem first shop.

Newer players (avoiding leap pvp) can consider auto-buying S2 and G2; avoid O4.

If on Subterra Sanctum or Churchmouse Streets first stage, consider
G1, G4 or special gem.

** D: Ultra-niche
Gems close too many future lines and don't do efficient damage at base kit.

It doesn't help either, Shadows like having Special or Defensive to reroll Shackles.

1. O3
   - If on legacy 2.0.1.3 (Steam) version, this and reworked O1 can carry games
     by itself
   - On CD usage:
     + N3 :: 60 DPS
     + O3 :: 67 DPS
     + N3 - N1 :: 6 DPS
     + O3 - N1 :: 53 DPS
   - On 4P, items are often contested by other classes, i.e.:
     + Timespace Dagger
     + Miner's Cap
     + Literally any non-% Special reset item like Heaven's Codex; and, even moreso,
       Shinobi Tabi, Raindrop Earrings, etc.
   - Disables G1/G2 lines and any % loot lines (~maybe 20% of chests in 4P)
2. E4
   - On CD usage:
     + N4 :: 24 DPS
     + E4 :: 45 DPS
     + N4 - N1 :: 10.7 DPS
     + E4 - N1 :: 35 DPS
   - On 4P, you lose prio to others with way more reliable lines, i.e.:
     + Firststrike Bracelet (Blackstrike)
     + Timespace Dagger
     + Miner's Cap
