** Sample Definitions
- {{{abls(N1)}}}, {{{gems(O1)}}}, {{{gems(S1)}}}, {{{gems(R1)}}}, {{{gems(E1)}}}, {{{gems(G1)}}} :: _Normal_, _Opal_, _Sapphire_, _Ruby_, _Emerald_ and _Garnet_ variants of Primary respectively.
- {{{gems(R1)}}}, {{{gems(R2)}}}, {{{gems(R3)}}}, {{{gems(R4)}}} :: _Ruby_ variants of each respectively: Primary, Secondary, Special and Defensive.
- BiS :: Best in Slot; universal best upgrade.
- AoE :: Area-of-Effect; typically used in context of hitting multiple enemies, e.g. fights in Churchouse or Sanctum.
- oGCD :: Shadow's S3, S4; these can be used without sacrificing GCD time spent on other abilities.
- iframe :: Invincibility
- prio :: priority; typically what item/gems should be picked and by whom

* Playstyle
** Base Kit
Maximum damage rotation would prioritize in order of availability.

\(N4 \to N3 \to N1 \to N1\)

More typically, for mobile targets, I save {{{abls(N3)}}} for when I'm out of range or shackled.
{{{abls(N4)}}} is good for burst,
i.e. on 4P Lunar against the unshielded butterfly on (Geode) Plee or (Sanctum) Capricorn.

As a rule-of-thumb, {{{abls(N1)}}}, {{{abls(N4)}}} and {{{abls(N2)}}} have about the same hitbox horizontally.
[fn:N2:Diameter of N1 and N4 is 5% bigger than the length of N2.]

* Gems Tier List
Tier placing prioritizes "lines" and WR consistency.
Shadow, by gem design, should buy a gem first gem first shop, most are damage positive.

- Newer players, if avoiding leap PvP, can consider auto-buying {{{gems(S2)}}} and {{{gems(G2)}}}; avoid {{{gems(O4)}}}.
- If on Subterra Sanctum or Churchmouse Streets first stage,
  consider {{{gems(G1)}}}, {{{gems(G4)}}} or special gem.
- {{{gems(O2)}}}, {{{gems(G3)}}} is great for MP, they're practically SS's Superhex and Dancer's Warcry respectively.
